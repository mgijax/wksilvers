Figure 3-7. Hair bulb of a light ( a/a;Blt/Blt) mouse (10th day of hair growth cycle). Approx., x550. In this bulb melanogenesis has ceased early, active melanocytes are absent from the hair bulb, and the epithelial cells lining the papilla cavity form a highly uniform border as they do in hair bulbs in white spotted areas (compare with Fig. 3-10 and 3-11).
