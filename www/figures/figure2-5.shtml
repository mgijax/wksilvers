From left to right: recessive yellow ( e/e) mouse which had been partially plucked, and 5 days later had received a series of 10 daily injections of MSH; lethal yellow ( Ay) mouse similarly treated; Ay mouse which had been plucked but which received medium rather than MSH; Ay mouse similarly treated but which received MSH only on days 8 and 9 after plucking.
