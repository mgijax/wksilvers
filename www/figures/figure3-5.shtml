Figure 3-5. On the left is a hair bulb of a light ( a/a;Blt/Blt) mouse (thirteenth day of hair growth cycle). Note that only two large, round melanocytes remain on one side of the papilla and that these cells lack well-developed dendritic processes. On the right is another light ( a/a;Blt/Blt) follicle (tenth day of hair growth cycle). This one has only one large unbranched melanocyte attached to the apex of the attenuated dermal papilla. Approx. x400. From E.P. McGrath and W.C. Quevedo ( 1965). Reproduced with permission of the authors and the Australian Academy of Science.
