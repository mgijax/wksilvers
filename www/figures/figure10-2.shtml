Figure 10-2. Dorsal and ventral views of Wf/Wf (left), Wf/+ (middle), and +/+ (right) mice. Note that while the Wf homozygote exhibits extensively depigmented areas lacking any well-defined pattern, the Wf/+ heterozygote (on the C3H/He background) is characterized by sharply limited white spots on the forehead and on the belly as well as a white tail tip. These spots are variable in size and sometimes reduced to a few white hairs. Courtesy of J.L. Guénet.
